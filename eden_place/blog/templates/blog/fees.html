How to pay

Payment of fees

We offer a variety of payment options for school fees. These include online payment, bank transfer, and cash or check payments. All payments must be made on time to ensure your child's enrollment and participation in school activities. For more information on fee structures and payment options, please visit us or contact our office directly.

Registration fees

We are excited to welcome new pupils to our school community! To register your child, please visit our website and fill out an online application form. A non-refundable admission fee is required at the time of registration. Additionally, you will be required to submit all necessary documents and pay the termly tuition fee. Please contact our admissions office for further information and assistance with the registration process.

Co-curricular fees

We believe in providing a well-rounded education for our pupils and offer a variety of co-curricular activities such as sports, music, art, and clubs. Participation in these activities may be optional and subject to an additional fee. The fees vary depending on the activity, for more information please contact our office directly.

Excursions

At our school, we believe that hands-on learning experiences are an important part of a child's education. That's why we offer a variety of excursions throughout the school year to give our pupils the opportunity to explore their interests and learn in new and exciting ways. Our excursions are designed to complement and enhance the curriculum in a fun and engaging way.

We understand that safety is of the utmost importance when it comes to excursions. Our staff carefully plans and supervises all excursions, and we ensure that all necessary precautions are taken to keep our pupils safe. Excursion fees are required to cover the cost of transportation, admission, and other expenses.

Siblings Discount

We understand that education is an investment for families and we strive to make it affordable for all. We believe that education is a right, not a privilege and we strive to make it accessible for all. That's why we offer a sibling discount for families with multiple children enrolled in our school. This discount applies to the annual tuition fee for each additional sibling and can significantly reduce the overall cost for families. To qualify for this discount, please contact our office for more information on how to register siblings together.
